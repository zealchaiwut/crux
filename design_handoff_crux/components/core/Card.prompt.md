The neutral surface container. Quiet by default; `lead` applies the violet wash that marks the leading plan or focused item.

```jsx
<Card>Plain content</Card>
<Card lead>Leading plan A — deload</Card>
<Card hover padding={20}>Hover-lift case row</Card>
```

- `lead`: violet border + `--crux-tint` wash (use sparingly — one per group)
- `hover`: enables the violet-tinted hover lift
- `padding`: inner padding px (default 16)
